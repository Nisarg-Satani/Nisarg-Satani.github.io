My Work

Smart Street Light

The main aim was to save electricity which is being wasted due to the unnecessary usage of electricity.

  • In this project components used were Arduino micro-controller, ultrasonic sensors, and LED's to demonstrate as street lights.
  • Arduino was programmed to turn on an LED. Whenever a vehicle comes near to the street light ultrasonic sensor will trigger the led to turn it on.
  • After when the vehicle passes the street light, the light will turn off automatically.

Mobile restriction in running vehicles

The aim was to avoid accidents which caused due to use of mobile.

  • The components used were the GSM module, Arduino, Motor driver, frequency synthesizer.
  • To demonstrate the project we made a car which when started it triggered that instrument that jams the signal and the driver cannot use mobile.
  • Went to Pandit Dindayal Petrolium University,Gandhinagar for SSIP Hackathon for final round.This project which was part of the Hackathon which was conducted by SSIP.
